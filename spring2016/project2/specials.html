List of Specials

Ashe Reshte

Reshteh (thin noodles), kashk (a whey-like, fermented dairy product), herbs such as parsley, spinach, dill, spring onion ends and sometimes coriander, chick peas, black eye beans, lentils, onions, flour, dried mint, garlic, oil, salt and pepper.

Kash O Bademjoon

Eggplants, onion, chopped mint(fresh or dried), turmeric, garlic, walnuts, Kashk (liquid whey protein).

Sholezard

Rice, sugar, saffron, butter, rosewater, cinnamon.

Bastani Sonati

Milk, eggs, sugar, rose water, saffron, vanilla, and pistachios. Salep is also sometimes included as an ingredient. Bastani often contains flakes of frozen clotted cream.

Falloodeh

Caster sugar, lemon juice, orange-blossom water or rosewater, rice vermicelli, sour cherry syrup or pomegranate molasses, pomegranate seeds.

Mast Moosir

Small Shallot, Middle Eastern Lebni or Plain (unsweetened) Greek Yogurt.

Dough

Savory yogurt-based beverage popular in Iran, Azerbaijan, It is sometimes carbonated and seasoned with mint.
